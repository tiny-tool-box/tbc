Lost Mail Redistribution Program

L
ost mail is a monthly visual art and narrative puzzle, running from 03.2018 until 02.2020. After about six months, each set is made public, linked below. For the rest, you will need a passcode to unlock both the story and all digital rewards, like wallpapers. While the project was running, its Patreon supporters received a passcode. Now that this part is completed, the PAtreon is suspended as we develop the next iteraction of this story. Thanks for all your support!
